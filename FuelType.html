Fuel Type Impact
 
Finally, we take a look at how fuel type is a factor in car mileage. If an electric car can travel 100 miles on 33.7 kWh of electricity, the EPA rates it at 100 MPGe. A gas car would have to travel 100 miles per gallon to be equivalent so electric cars are much more efficient. The box plot compares all three: gasoline, diesel, and electricity. Points are included as well to emphasize sample size.
